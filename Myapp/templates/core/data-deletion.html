Request for Data Deletion

Last Updated: July 16, 2025

At NyumbaChap, we believe in transparency and your right to control your personal information. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal data from our systems.

Who Can Request Deletion?

Any registered user of NyumbaChap can request for their account or data to be permanently removed from our systems.

How to Submit a Request

  1. Send an email to support@nyumbachap.com using the same email address you used to register.
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. In your message, include:
    • Your full name
    • Your NyumbaChap username (if applicable)
    • A short confirmation message, e.g., “I confirm that I want my data to be deleted from NyumbaChap.”

What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your request:

  • We will verify your identity for security purposes
  • Your account and associated data will be deleted within 7 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Important Notes

  • Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or regulatory compliance
  • Deleted accounts cannot be restored
  • You will lose access to any services linked to your account

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, don’t hesitate to contact us at support@nyumbachap.com
